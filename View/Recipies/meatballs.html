Meatballs!

Ingredients

  • meatballs 500 g
  • SQUISHED TATERS! 1KG
  • BROWNSAUCE 1L
  • lingon berries 500 g

Directives

  1. BOIL DEM TATERS
  2. MASH 'EM, STICK 'EM IN A STEW
  3. FRY DEM BALLERS
  4. put on plate gently...
  5. POUR THAT SAUCE on taters precious
  6. POUR DEM LINGENBERRIES ALL OVER
